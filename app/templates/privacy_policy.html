By using ReviewGrow, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

When you create or use a ReviewGrow account, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Password in securely hashed form
  • Business name
  • Business category
  • Business contact details
  • Account role and permissions
  • Subscription and account status
  • Login and authentication information

We do not store your account password in plain text.

1.2 Google account and Google Business Profile data

When you connect a Google Business Profile through Google OAuth, we may receive or access information permitted by the Google scopes you approve, including:

  • Your Google account email address and identifiers
  • Google Business Profile accounts and business locations
  • Business names, addresses, contact information, Profile metadata, and connection status
  • Customer reviews, ratings, reviewer names, publicly available reviewer information, review dates, and content
  • Existing business responses and information needed to submit an approved response
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to maintain the connection

ReviewGrow accesses Google data only after you authorize access through Google’s consent screen. We do not receive or store your Google account password.

1.3 Review and reputation data

We may process:

  • Reviews retrieved from connected Google Business Profiles
  • Reviews or feedback uploaded manually, including CSV or spreadsheet data
  • Review replies and AI-generated reply suggestions
  • Sentiment results, complaints, praises, and business insights
  • Reports, review trends, and aggregated statistics

Manually uploaded review data and live Google Business Profile review data may be processed separately depending on the feature being used.

1.4 AI processing information

When you use an AI-powered feature, ReviewGrow may send relevant information to an AI service provider such as Gemini, including review text, ratings, business category, business name or context when required, and instructions used to generate summaries, insights, or suggested replies.

We seek to send only information reasonably necessary for the requested feature. AI-generated information may be inaccurate or incomplete. Users are responsible for reviewing AI-generated replies, reports, and recommendations before relying on or publishing them.

1.5 WhatsApp review request information

When a business user sends or schedules a review request through an available WhatsApp integration, we may process:

  • Customer name and telephone number
  • Business name and review link
  • Message template, status, date, and time
  • Delivery-related information provided by the messaging provider

The business user is responsible for ensuring it has the customer’s consent or another valid legal basis to contact the customer. ReviewGrow must not be used for unsolicited, deceptive, or unlawful messages.

1.6 Payment and subscription information

When paid subscriptions are available, we may collect or receive the selected plan, billing status, payment amount, transaction identifier, payment date, subscription dates, payment-provider response, and invoice or tax information where applicable.

Full card, bank, or UPI credentials are generally processed by the applicable provider and are not intended to be stored directly by ReviewGrow.

1.7 Technical and usage information

We may automatically collect IP address, browser and device information, operating system, login timestamps, pages and features used, diagnostic information, security and audit events, approximate IP-derived location, referring URLs, and session and cookie information.

We may use information to:

  • Create, maintain, and authenticate accounts
  • Protect accounts and prevent fraud, misuse, unauthorized access, and security incidents
  • Connect authorized Google Business Profiles and retrieve and display reviews
  • Generate summaries, sentiment analysis, insights, suggested replies, and downloadable reports
  • Post a reply only after an authorized user takes the required approval or submission action
  • Send authorized review requests
  • Administer subscriptions and payments
  • Provide support, diagnose errors, and improve performance and user experience
  • Enforce our Terms, comply with law, maintain records, and resolve disputes

We do not sell personal data.

Google information is used only to provide or improve user-facing ReviewGrow features visible and relevant to the user who authorized access.

We do not use Google user data to sell information, advertise, create advertising profiles, determine creditworthiness, conduct surveillance, or for purposes unrelated to authorized ReviewGrow features. Access is limited to authorized systems, service providers, and personnel who require it to operate, protect, or support the service.

ReviewGrow will submit a response only when an authorized business user has approved or initiated it, or has expressly enabled a clearly disclosed automation feature where legally and contractually permitted.
